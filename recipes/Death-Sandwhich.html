Death Sandwich

Description

The Death Sandwich is a fictional sandwich from the Cartoon Network animated series The Regular Show. It is said to be the most powerful sandwich in the world, and anyone who eats it will gain immense strength and power. However, it is also said to be very dangerous, and eating it can have serious consequences.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 jar of jelly
  • 1 pound of steak
  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 1 pound of sausage
  • 1 pound of ham
  • 1 dozen eggs
  • 1 bottle of hot sauce
  • 1 gallon of chili
  • 1 pound of cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Spread peanut butter on one slice of toast and jelly on the other slice.
  4. Cook the steak, bacon, sausage, and ham.
  5. Scramble the eggs.
  6. Open the bottle of hot sauce and pour it over the sandwich.
  7. Add the chili, cheese, and cooked meat to the sandwich.
  8. Eat the sandwich.

    9. Result

    If you followed all the steps correctly, you should have created the most powerful sandwich in the world!
